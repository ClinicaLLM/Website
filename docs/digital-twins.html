The Living Digital Replica

A Digital Twin is more than just a model; it's a dynamic virtual representation of a physical asset, process, or system. In healthcare, this could be a patient's physiology, a surgical procedure, or even an entire hospital workflow.

Fueled by real-time data streams (like EHRs, IoT sensors, imaging data), the Digital Twin continuously updates, reflecting the state of its physical counterpart. This allows for sophisticated analysis, simulation, and prediction without impacting the real world.

  • Real-time data integration.
  • Continuous synchronization.
  • Simulation and 'what-if' analysis.
  • Predictive modeling based on live data.
